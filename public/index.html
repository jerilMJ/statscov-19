StatsCOV-19

The progress in flattening the curve, visualized


StatsCOV-19 is an app available for Android that brings to you the data provided by John Hopkins University (source: CSSEGISandData/COVID-19) in easily viewable forms with well-known visualization techniques and charts. Features include:


  • View reports of any country individually
  • Compare multiple reports together
  • View the latest global report
  • See how the pandemic spread on the map
  • View status offline once downloaded (from cached data)
  • Pin reports to access them quicker
  • No permissions required
